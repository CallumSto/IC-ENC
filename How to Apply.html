How to Apply

Following the success of the three IC-ENC ENC Validation Training Courses held in the UK, Australia and Panama during 2015, we will be holding three further courses during 2016.

If you would like to attend one of our training courses in 2016, please express your interest by completing an initial questionnaire, and then send your completed questionnaire to Mike Hawes, IC-ENC Course Trainer/Data Manager, on Mike.Hawes@ic-enc.org.

Click here​​ to download the IC-ENC ENC Validation training course questionnaire.

More than one application can be submitted from each member HO. However, IC-ENC has received a high level of interest in the course, so participants will be selected to ensure that all members can benefit as soon as possible.

Once dates for the two additional courses are finalised, applicants will be contacted with details regarding attendance.​​

For more information about the ENC Validation training course, please contact Mike Hawes.​

Mission:
IC-ENC aims to provide services, at a low cost, to national Hydrographic Offices that ensure their ENCs are compliant to the international standards, consistent across the global dataset, and readily available for use. This is so that shipping can navigate safely, efficiently and confidently, whilst ensuring other maritime users are using the same approved data.
